Mars

about mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is the second smallest planet in the solar system. Named after the Roman god of war. Mars is sometimes called the Red Planet. It's red because of rusty iron in the ground. Like Earth, Mars has seasons, polar ice caps, volcanoes, canyons, and weather. It has a very thin atmosphere made of carbon dioxide, nitrogen, and argon. the average temperature on Mars is about -63c.
